The Anatomy of Grey’s Anatomy

Bio:

Are you interested in drama, love, handsome surgeons, and a hospital where the doctors drop faster than the patients? Well, do I have a show for you! Grey’s Anatomy is based of the life of general surgeon Meredith Grey and her wild life at Grey-Sloan Memorial Hospital. We also embark on the journeys of her friends and lovers. Here we will dissect into the anatomy of our favorite show, Grey’s Anatomy.
